Cookies & Garden Bites Policy

We use digital breadcrumbs — just like compost enriches soil — to improve your journey. Read how we respect your privacy while you explore our horticultural resources.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit Verdant Vistas. They help us remember your preferences (like language or garden zone), analyze visitor trends, and make our website more functional — much like a well-planned crop rotation. We never sell your data; cookies just help our digital garden flourish.

This policy explains the types of cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can manage your consent. By using our site, you agree to the use of cookies as described, unless you adjust your browser settings or use our consent tool below.

Manage your cookie preferences

You’re in control. You can change your cookie settings at any time using our consent manager below, or through your browser settings. Please note: disabling essential cookies may affect site functionality (e.g., contact form submission or remembering your preferences).

You can also manage cookies directly in your browser: visit Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies. Deleting cookies will reset your preferences.

Third-party services & embedded content

Our gardening sanctuary may include embedded videos (YouTube), social media feeds, and interactive maps (OpenStreetMap). These services may set their own cookies. We only include them after ensuring they align with eco-conscious values. For more details, refer to:

  • Google/YouTube Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Cookie Policy
  • Google Analytics — we use IP anonymization and data retention controls.

How long do we store cookies?

Session cookies expire when you close your browser. Persistent cookies have durations ranging from 30 days up to 2 years (for analytics). We regularly review and purge obsolete cookies. Our functional preferences (like garden zone reminder) last 12 months unless you clear them.

If you have any questions regarding our cookie practices, please email privacy@verdantvistas.com or use our contact page.
